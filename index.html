What Is The Chest?

The Chest is a shop in the mall on Javasurvival. If you don't know what Javasurvival is, then I'm not sure how you got here in the first place. All sorts of items are sold there, but more recently, plans have been made to sell kits. This website was made to make it easier to see what is in the kits.
